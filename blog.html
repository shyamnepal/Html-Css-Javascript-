Effect of the technology The branch of knowledge dealing with engineering or applied science is called technology. The word technology is a combination of two Greek word, techne and logos. Techne means art, craft, or skill. Logos means to speak of. Innovation of the new things which is beneficial to the human is good things but if we forgot that our environment and the surroundings is more important than the innovation it cause serious negative effects in our lives. On the current situation of the world it is dependent to the technology. Every person of the world is related or depend upon the technology. There are many types of technology which are being used in this. Technology have its own pros and cons but the advantages of the technology is more in compare to the dis-advantages. Technology such are many kinds of wireless and other devices are helping people and make their work easier and faster. All the manner of the technology surrounds us. But there are many problem regard the technology which make our society irresponsible. There is huge improvement and expanding of the technology in short period of time. Modern technology has given people many freedom and power to do many things. But people in our society are using it in wrong manner. They are using it in bad prospective which make bad impression of modern technology in society. There are many technology which are very important such are the technology used in health sector, education and many other places. In this time most of the places are using the modern technology to complete their work. Technology helps us on many aspects of our daily lives.
